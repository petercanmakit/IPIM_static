Mapping tool

Welcome to I-PIM, a crowd-sourcing research project from Columbia University that will help to reduce the number of pedestrian injuries and fatalities cause by car collisions. We hope to understand what aspects of streets and intersections put pedestrians at risk of being hit by automobiles. We would like you to use a mapping tool to provide us with information about how and where you were injured while out walking.

Step 1

Use the mouse to place the pin at the place where you were hit by a vehicle.

Step 2

Use the pencil tool to draw lines on the map showing us the streets you walked along before you were hit.

Step 3

Please tell us a little more about yourself as well as the accident. If you are a signed up user, your information is already stored.
