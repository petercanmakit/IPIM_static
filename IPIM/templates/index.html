Welcome to I-PIM!

Interactive Pedestrian Injury Mapper

Welcome! We’re sorry you were hit by a car, but we hope you telling us about your experience will help prevent future collisions.

We will ask you to place a pin at the location where you were hit by a car and then map out the route you were walking up to the place where you were injured. Then we will ask you a few questions about what happened. We will use your story to figure out which features of the street where you were hit made it dangerous, and then try to get those features changed!

Before we can record any information about you or your experience, we will need your consent to participate in our research project. Press the “Start I-PIM” button to see the official consent form and begin telling us your story.

Start I-PIM

Step 1

Use the mouse to place the pin at the place where you were hit by a vehicle.

Step 2

Use the cross-hairs tool to map out the route you walked along before you were hit.

Step 3

Tell us a little more about yourself as well as the accident.
